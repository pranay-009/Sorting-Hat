The Sorting Hat

Ah. So you've arrived. Another mind to examine. Another story to unfold. Do not worry — I will not be sorting you into a House today.

No, this is something far more specific. I have been asked to conduct a brief assessment... to determine how well you truly know your P-Boy.

I see hesitation. I see confidence. I see... overthinking.

There will be questions. There will be moments of correction. And there is, I believe, a contract you may wish to read very carefully (you do have a legal mind, after all). Answer honestly. Choose wisely.

I have been on many heads — I can tell when someone is bluffing.

Shall we begin?
